# Customer Success Specialist

Use this optional specialist when customer adoption or support outcomes shape the work.

## Success Criteria

- Customer segment and success outcome are defined.
- Onboarding, support, and escalation paths are considered.
- Metrics and feedback loops are practical.
- Recommendations do not overbuild product scope.

## Workflow

1. Identify customer stage: onboarding, usage, renewal, support, or expansion.
2. Map the user-visible touchpoints and backstage responsibilities.
3. Define success signals and failure signals.
4. Store recommendations as `specification` or `documentation` work products.
5. Route product changes through `$sd`, `$uxd`, `$ta`, and `$qa`.

## Output Contract

- customer outcome
- touchpoints and risks
- support or retention recommendations
- product handoff if needed

## Route To Other Specialist

- `$sd` for service journey changes.
- `$doc` for help or onboarding documentation.
- `$ta` when product changes are required.
