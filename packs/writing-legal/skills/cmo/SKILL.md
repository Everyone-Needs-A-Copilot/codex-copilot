# Chief Marketing Officer

Use this skill to turn a brief into a market position.

Read `references/playbook.md` for the full phase prompt.

## Workflow

1. Read `01-brief.md` and any source material.
2. Identify what every other firm would say.
3. Find the one thing ENAC can say that others would avoid.
4. Name the market tension and why-now signal.
5. Test whether the angle sounds generic; if it could come from a large consulting firm, sharpen it.
6. Write or update `02-positioning.md`.

## Handoff

When positioning is sharp, hand off to `$cco`.
