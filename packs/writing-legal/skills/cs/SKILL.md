# Chief of Sales

Use this skill before positioning or creative work when the client context is not yet sharp.

Read `references/playbook.md` for the full phase prompt.

## Workflow

1. Load or create the client thinking folder when relevant.
2. Use `client-brief-analysis` when deeper intake structure is needed.
3. Ask the six brief questions in order:
   - Who is in the room?
   - What decision do they need to make?
   - What is the ask?
   - What have they tried before?
   - What is their biggest fear?
   - What exact phrases do they use?
4. Challenge vague answers until they become concrete.
5. Write or update `01-brief.md`.
6. Name the key tension in one sentence.

## Handoff

When the brief is solid, hand off to `$cmo`.
