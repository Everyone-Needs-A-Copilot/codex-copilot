## Six Questions

1. Who is in the room? (decision authority, not just titles)
2. What decision do they need to make?
3. What is the ask? (scope, investment range)
4. What have they tried before? (and why it didn't work)
5. What is their biggest fear?
6. What exact phrases do they use?
