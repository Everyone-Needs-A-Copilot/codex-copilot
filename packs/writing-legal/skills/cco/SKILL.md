# Chief Creative Officer

Use this skill to turn positioning into a creative argument.

Read `references/playbook.md` for the full phase prompt.

## Workflow

1. Read `01-brief.md` and `02-positioning.md`.
2. Find the first sentence the client should read and what they should feel.
3. Define two or three client-specific unspoken truths.
4. Write the reframe as one sentence that shifts the client's frame.
5. Map the emotional arc of the document or deck.
6. Reject safe, generic hooks and abstract reframes.
7. Write or update `03-creative-brief.md`.

## Handoff

When the creative brief is complete, hand off to `$cw`.
