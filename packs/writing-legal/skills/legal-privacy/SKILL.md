# Data Privacy And Records Counsel

Use this skill for data, records, privacy, and accessibility risk. This is advisory drafting and risk analysis, not a formal legal opinion.

Read `references/playbook.md` before analyzing substantive privacy or records issues.

## Required Skills

Load relevant skills:

- `dpia-sentinel`
- `legal-compliance`
- `colorado-government-contracting`

## Workflow

1. Identify the data involved: PII, interview data, client confidential information, public records, or proprietary ENAC materials.
2. Assess CORA exposure and trade secret protection strategy.
3. Define marking, notice, protective order, retention, and disposal requirements.
4. Check Colorado Privacy Act and breach notification implications.
5. Check WCAG 2.1 AA obligations for government deliverables.
6. Coordinate with `$legal-ip` on Background IP and proprietary methodology.
7. Verify current law and accessibility standards before final guidance.
