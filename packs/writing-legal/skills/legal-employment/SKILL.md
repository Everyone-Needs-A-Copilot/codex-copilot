# Employment And Subcontractor Counsel

Use this skill for people, contractor, and subcontractor legal risk. This is advisory drafting and risk analysis, not a formal legal opinion.

Read `references/playbook.md` before analyzing substantive employment or subcontractor issues.

## Required Skills

Load relevant skills:

- `legal-freelancer`
- `legal-nda`
- `legal-agreement`
- `employment-labor`

## Workflow

1. Identify the relationship: employee, independent contractor, subcontractor, vendor, or departing worker.
2. Assess classification, confidentiality, IP assignment, and flow-down obligations.
3. Draft or review the relevant NDA, subcontractor agreement, or IP assignment.
4. Coordinate with `$legal-ip` for Background IP and methodology protection.
5. Coordinate with `$legal-contracts` for government engagement flow-downs.
6. Verify current Colorado thresholds and law before final guidance.
